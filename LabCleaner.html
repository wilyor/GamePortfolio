LabCleaner

Videogame made for the EVAD's master of game design and development in 2023. Made in Unity. In this third person view game you control a laboratory worker who has the labor of clean the facility, but that day a lot of experiments scape for a security breach. In this game you have a water gun that has four shooting styles, Water, laser, ice and steam, each one with different interactions with the environment.

In this game, the main character and the enemies can get statuses like burnt, icy and drown. There are multiple enemies, each one with different AI behaviours.
